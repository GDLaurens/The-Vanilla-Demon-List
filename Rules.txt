# The Vanilla Demon List
 
## Official Rulebook – Version 1.0
 
---
 
## 1. Overview
 
The Vanilla Demon List (VDL) ranks the hardest demons completed on the official, unmodified version of Geometry Dash.
 
This list exists to measure mechanical skill under official client conditions only.
 
No external modifications, injected tools, or gameplay-altering utilities are permitted.
 
All accepted records must strictly follow the rules outlined below.
 
---
 
## 2. Client Requirements
 
All completions must be achieved using:
 
- The official Steam release of Geometry Dash
- Default game physics
- No external client modifications
 
### 2.1 Prohibited Modifications
 
The following are strictly forbidden:
 
- MegaHack (any version)
- Geode
- Any DLL injection
- Any external gameplay-altering tools
- Clickbot / macros / automation
- Startpos Switcher
- Texture packs or modified assets
- Custom keybind programs
- Overlays (Discord overlay, Nvidia overlay, RTSS, etc.)
- Ultrawide resolutions (21:9 or wider)
- Modified physics of any kind
- Any modded or third-party implementations of Click Between Frames (CBF)
 
Use of any prohibited modification will result in record rejection and possible removal from the list.
 
---
 
## 3. Allowed Features
 
The following official in-game features are permitted:
 
- Built-in FPS Bypass (in-game setting only)
- Built-in in-game Click Between Frames (CBF) only
- Practice Music Sync (In Game setting only)
- Controller gameplay
- Default graphics settings
- Standard 16:9 or 16:10 resolutions
- Windowed or fullscreen mode
 
No third-party equivalents of these features are allowed.
 
---
 
## 4. FPS & Click Between Frames (CBF) Policy
 
### 4.1 FPS Bypass
 
- Only the official in-game FPS bypass setting is allowed.
- No third-party FPS tools or external frame manipulation software may be used.
- Game physics must remain at default values.
 
FPS bypass is permitted because it is an official built-in feature of Geometry Dash.
 
---
 
### 4.2 Click Between Frames (CBF)
 
- Only the official in-game CBF setting is allowed.
- No modded, injected, or third-party CBF implementations are permitted.
 
CBF usage is restricted as follows:
 
- Players running below 120 FPS may enable CBF.
- Players running at or above 120 FPS may not enable CBF.
 
Violation of this rule will result in record rejection.
 
---
 
## 5. Resolution Policy
 
- Only standard 16:9 or 16:10 aspect ratios are allowed.
- Ultrawide (21:9 or wider) is prohibited.
- Custom stretched or manipulated resolutions are not allowed.
 
---
 
## 6. Proof Requirements
 
All records must include full, uncut raw footage. **Audible clicks or taps must be clearly heard throughout the entire completion.** Edited, clipped, or segmented recordings will not be accepted.
 
---
 
### 6.1 Submission Evidence (Post-Completion)
 
To ensure the integrity of the run, the following must be shown in the same continuous recording immediately following the level completion:
 
1. **In-Game Settings:** Open the settings menu and show all relevant tabs (specifically FPS Bypass and CBF settings).
2. **Game Files:** Close the game and show the Geometry Dash game files directory, scrolling through the folder contents to prove no external DLLs or mod loaders (like Geode) are present.
3. **Re-Verification:** Re-launch the game from Steam and briefly show the settings one final time to ensure no changes occurred during the closing process.
 
Raw footage must be provided upon request.
 
---
 
## 7. Verification & Review
 
The VDL moderation team reserves the right to:
 
- Request additional proof at any time.
- Conduct periodic file checks for high-ranking players.
- Deny any record that appears suspicious.
- Remove records if new evidence arises.
 
Even if a submission technically meets listed requirements, it may be rejected if credibility cannot be established.
 
All moderation decisions are final.
 
---
 
## 8. Enforcement
 
Failure to follow these rules may result in:
 
- Record rejection
- Record removal
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent ban from the list
 
The integrity of the Vanilla Demon List takes priority over individual records.
 
---
 
## 9. Philosophy
 
The Vanilla Demon List exists to measure performance under official client conditions only.
 
This list is not anti-mod — it is pro-vanilla competition.
